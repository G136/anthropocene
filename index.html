Redistribution of Resources During the Anthropocene

Overall formula

\[ \textnormal{Offset}_c = \left( \frac{V_c}{\sum_{j=1}^n V_j} - \frac{I_c}{\sum_{j=1}^n I_j} \right) \]
  • Offset\( _c \) is the % of resources a given country country \( c \) contributes (+) or reveives (-).
  • \(V_c\) is the index of vulnerability for a given country \( c \), from a 3rd party source.
  • \(I_c\) is the index of responsibility for a given country \( c \), calculated as per below.

Index of Responsibility (modified)

\[ I_c = \frac {\textnormal{CO}_2 (c)} {\, R_c} - 1 \]
  • CO\( _2 \) \( (c) \) are the actual emissions of CO\( _2 \) for a given country \( c \),
  • \(R_c\) represents the modeled emissions of CO\( _2 \) for a given country \( c \).

Factor analysis

\[ R_c = (\lambda_1 a_1 + \lambda_2 a_2 +\lambda_3 a_3) \cdot \textnormal{CO}_2(w) \] \[ a_1 = \frac {\sum_{t= \textnormal{hist} }^{t=0} \textnormal{GDP}_c} {\sum_{t=\textnormal{hist}}^{t=0} \textnormal{GDP}_w} \] \[ a_2 = \frac {\sum_{t= \textnormal{hist} }^{t=0} \textnormal{Population}_c} {\sum_{t=\textnormal{hist}}^{t=0} \textnormal{Population}_w} \] \[ a_3 = \frac {\sum_{t= \textnormal{hist} }^{t=0} \textnormal{Energy}_c} {\sum_{t=\textnormal{hist}}^{t=0} \textnormal{Energy}_w} \]
  • \(\textnormal{CO}_2(w)\) are the world carbon emissions
  • We use Machine Learning Linear regression to calculate \( \lambda_i \), so that the error is the smallest.
